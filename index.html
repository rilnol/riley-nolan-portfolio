Our Story

Built by family.
Shared with all.

Deep in the heart of Transylvania County, surrounded by the ancient ridges of the Blue Ridge Mountains, The Bar-N stands as a testament to the Nolan family's love of the land — and of bringing people together.

Ben Nolan and the Nolan family have cultivated this property into one of the most breathtaking event destinations in Western North Carolina. From golden summer sunsets that paint the sky in fire to crisp autumn mornings when the mountains blush red and orange, The Bar-N offers a backdrop that no ballroom can rival.

Whether you're celebrating a once-in-a-lifetime wedding, gathering your clan for a family reunion, or simply casting a line in the pond on a quiet afternoon — you're not just visiting a place. You're becoming part of its story.

"The best things in life happen when you open your barn doors and let people in."

Location

Lake Toxaway,
North Carolina

Nestled in Transylvania County at over 3,000 feet elevation, Lake Toxaway is one of the most scenically preserved communities in the entire Appalachian region. The Bar-N sits amid this natural splendor — close enough to be convenient, remote enough to feel like an escape.

The drive in winds through mountain roads flanked by hardwood forests, arriving to an open sky that seems to go on forever. Come at golden hour and you'll understand why this place is so hard to leave.
